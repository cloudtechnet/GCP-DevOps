## VM Lifecycle Management

In **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**, **VM Lifecycle Management** refers to the **complete set of stages and operations** involved in managing a virtual machine from **creation to deletion**.

It ensures **availability, cost control, automation, and reliability** of VM-based workloads.
