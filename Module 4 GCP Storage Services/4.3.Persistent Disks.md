## Persistent Disks

In **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**, **Persistent Disks (PDs)** are **durable block storage devices** used with **Compute Engine VMs**. They provide reliable, high-performance storage that **persists independently of VM lifecycle**.

## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Zonal vs Regional Persistent Disks

### üîπ Zonal Persistent Disks

**Characteristics:**

* Located in **one zone**
* Attached to VMs in the same zone
* Lower cost
* Single-zone availability

**Best For:**

* Non-critical workloads
* Dev/Test
* Cost-sensitive applications

---

### üîπ Regional Persistent Disks

**Characteristics:**

* Synchronously replicated across **two zones** in the same region
* High availability
* Automatic failover support
* Higher cost than zonal disks

**Best For:**

* Mission-critical workloads
* Databases requiring high availability
* Production systems

---

| Cost         | Lower       | Higher         |
| Use Case     | Dev/Test    | Production, HA |
